Type R - The “R” stands for Racing.

The Type R brand delivers speed and excitement that stimulates the driver and enhances the driving experience. All Type R cars are immersed in Honda’s racing DNA and the knowledge and expertise of Honda’s engineers. When creating a Type R car, Honda aim to create the ultimate driving machine.

The spirit of Type R was first seen in 1964, long before the first Type R model was built. This was the year that Honda first joined Formula One. At the time Honda was still a relatively small car manufacturer but with the dedication of our engineers we met challenging targets and achieved what often seemed unachievable.

The first Honda Formula One machine to compete in a Grand Prix (Germany) was painted in Ivory White and featured the Honda emblem in red…these colours have been passed on to all Honda Type R cars.
BTCC
British Touring
Car Championship
Since the 2014 season, all cars have been built to the same NGTC regulations. First intriduced in 2011 for an initil five-year period, these technical regulations are designed to dramatically reduce the design, build and running costs of the cars and engines. Only NGTC cars are now eligible to compete in the BTCC. TOCA extended these regulations for a further six year from 2016.

NGTC allows independent teams to compete on a level playing field against manufacturer-backed efforts by keeping costs down whilst at the same time rewarding precise engineering and all-important split-second strategies.

Honda the Power of Dreams. I've achieved my Dream. Drive a Honda and you'll never go back.

Nick Tattoos

It is over all a great car, I have the 2009 championship edition, and it truly is one of the few cars that truly gives you what you want out of it, I’ve been on the Honda road for 7 years now, and have owned 7 Honda’s. From the old 1997 civic vtec, to the 2008 CRV, I also had a S2000 which I really regret selling. Two of my old civics have been in car magazines, the most recent one making it on the cover of speed and sound last year. I have owned: 2 1997 civic vtecs, 2 accord type S's, CRV, S2000 and my current Type R.

This type R is my 1st type R, but I am already planning to buy another, I have had nothing but joy out of the car, it offers everything you need, from the day to day luxuries (steering control, climate control and etc.), the ride of the car is fantastic, it truly makes you feel like you are driving more than just a daily car to go to work and back in, even the drivers seating position makes you feel like you are piloting something! The only thing I wish it had more of is power, but when you compare it to its rivals like the VW GTI; you quickly realize you actually have a lot more in your hands, considering they are turbo charged. The type R being naturally aspirated offers great power compared to the GTI which is turbo charged. I will never drive anything other than a Honda; the reliability and overall quality by far out ways any other car!

Thank you Honda for making real cars :D
